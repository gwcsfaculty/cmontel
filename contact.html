A note to applicants to the Machine Learning group at GW:  Thank you for your interest. Due to the high volume of email I receive, I will not be able to respond to queries individually.


Postdocs: If and when I am looking for postdocs, I will announce it, and make every effort to respond to serious queries that clearly demonstrate shared research interests.


PhD candidates: You should apply directly to the Computer Science PhD program through GW’s School of Engineering and Applied Science admissions page. If you are interested in working with me, please indicate this on your application. Applications are handled by an admissions committee. When looking for students, I will consider applicants whose preparation and research interests are strongly related to work in my group, however I will not be able reply individually, nor comment on your admissions chances.


Masters candidates: For any questions about admission to the Masters program, please contact SEAS admissions.


Current GW students and postdocs at all levels are welcome to contact me. Typically I only consider students for research after they have taken a course with me.


Miscellaneous: summer internships, visiting positions. It is very unlikely/rare for me to host such positions.
